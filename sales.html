Built for the messy middle.

Most tools assume life is already organised. Separation is the opposite: one income, two households, moving parts with the kids, and zero spare headspace. Atlas starts from that reality and gives you a calmer place to make decisions.

1
Concrete before inspirational Start with finances, kids week, and a small set of practical goals. Clarity comes before optimisation.
2
Phase-aware, not generic The right focus changes over time. Survive, stabilise, rebuild, and new chapter each call for different money, parenting, and planning decisions.
3
Built around the custody cycle Kids Week is not side admin. Handover days, school logistics, meals, screen time, and reminders all change the shape of the week.
4
Private by design Finances, Kids Week, and Goals all run privately in your browser. No account, no sync, and no data leaving your device.
Privacy

Your data stays yours.

Atlas is a quiet page in your browser, not a service. Nothing you enter — your income, your kids' week, your goals — leaves your device. There's no account, no sync, no servers reading your numbers.

In your browser
Local only.
Everything you enter is stored in your browser's local memory. Atlas never sends it anywhere — there's nowhere to send it.
No account
No sign‑up.
No login, no email, no password. Atlas doesn't know who you are, and doesn't need to.
You stay in control
Wipe it any time.
Clear your browser data and Atlas forgets — instantly. No copy on a server, no trail to chase.
Atlas is a tool, not a business in your data. There's no tracking pixel, no analytics, no ad network sitting on top of your separation.
